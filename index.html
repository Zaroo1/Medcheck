About MedCheck GH

MedCheck GH is a pharmacy-focused decision support tool developed to improve safe dispensing and pharmaceutical care in Ghanaian community pharmacies.

  • ✔ Improves patient safety
  • ✔ Supports clinical decision-making
  • ✔ Designed for low-resource settings
  • ✔ Offline-capable

Project Team (Rx31)

This project was developed by a 5-member pharmacy student team.

  2. Research & Interaction Database
